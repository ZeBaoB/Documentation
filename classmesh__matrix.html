Parameters
TMaturity time of the option.
s0Initial spot price of the underlying asset.
KStrike price of the option.
spot_mest_parameterNumber of spot price grid points (columns).
time_mesh_parameterNumber of time grid points (rows).
sinfMaximum spot price (boundary condition).

Initializes a mesh_matrix as a time_mesh_parameter x spot_mest_parameter grid. The parameters define the financial and numerical characteristics of the option pricing problem.
